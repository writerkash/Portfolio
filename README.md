### About

Writing is more than a profession for me. It is a craft I genuinely enjoy. My passion for documentation, structured content, and user-focused communication continues to drive my growth as a Technical Writer.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering and bring extensive experience in creating high-quality technical documentation for Enterprise SaaS and on-premises products. I enjoy learning new technologies, simplifying complex concepts, and enhancing user experience through clear, structured, and impactful documentation.

Over the years, I have contributed as a Technical Writer, Content Lead, Mentor, Trainer, and Project Manager, while actively driving documentation modernization initiatives such as DITA migration, content architecture revamp, localization workflows, and process standardization.
